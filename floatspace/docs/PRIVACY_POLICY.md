# Privacy Policy — FloatSpace

**Last Updated:** March 6, 2026

## Overview

FloatSpace ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required for the service:

- **Email address** — For Google OAuth login only
- **Profile name** — Displayed in study rooms and direct messages
- **Temporary chat messages** — Stored only while active in conversations

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate your account via Google OAuth
- To display your identity in chat rooms and messages
- To facilitate real-time messaging with other users

## Data Retention

**We do NOT retain data after you leave:**
- Messages are auto-deleted (default: 24 hours or upon session end)
- User profiles are deleted upon account termination
- We keep no backup of deleted messages
- Chat history is not archived

## Third-Party Services

FloatSpace uses:
- **Google OAuth** — For authentication (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
- **Google Apps Script** — Backend processing
- **Google Sheets** — Temporary data storage only

We do not share your data with third parties.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Request deletion of your account and all data
- Export your chat history before deletion
- Opt out of data collection by not using the service

## Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission. Chat messages are stored temporarily in Google Sheets and auto-deleted per your settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
